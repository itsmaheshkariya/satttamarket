RAISE KARKE KHELNE WALON KE LIYE..


Created: 2.11 10:42 by Admin Sir
Views: 85535

Is trick ke anusar ye hota hai ki kisi bhi din

Kalyan- Milan day- Main Mumbai- Milan night

ko milakar 8 ank nahi aate hai..


Ye tip unke liye hai jo daily khelte hai aur raise karte huye khel sakte hai.

Isme ye karna hai ki-
Sabse pahle milan day open aur kalyan open ka wait karna hai.
jab ye dono open aa jaye to yaha se khelna shuru karna hai......

manlo kalyan open aaya 1 aur milan open aaya 2
tab
ye dono ank milan close me khelne hai .
pass hone par us din aur nahi khelana hai...

manlo pass nahi hua aur milan close aaya 3 to
ab ank ho gaye .... 1 2 3
ab 1 2 3 ye kalyan close khelna hai pass hota hai to aage nahi khelna hai..
manlo pass nahi hua aur kalyan close aaya 4...
To ab milan night open me ank ho jayenge 1 2 3 4 ye khelne hai.

YAHA PAR BID DOUBLE KARNI HAI !

Milan night open me 1 2 3 4 khelne par pass ho jayega.
manlo pass nahi hua aur 5 aaya. tab
main open me ank ho jayenge 1 2 3 4 5

YAHA PAR BHI BID DOUBLE KARNI HAI

Pass hoga hi
nahi hua to aaya hua ank aur ye 5 ank matalab 6 ank milan night close me.. aise hi aage

Ab tak sabse late MILAN NIGHT CLOSE me pass huaa

*** jis din kalyan aur milan day open same ho us din nahi khelna hai aur SATURDAY ko nahi khelna hai.

NEVER FAIL TRICK HAI !
6 mahino ke record se match karne par kahi bhi fail nahi huyi hai.
