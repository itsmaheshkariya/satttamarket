CASE I:

Q. JO WEEK BAND HAI KYA USE GINANA HAI...???

Aksar guessers mujhe call karke puchhate hai ki jo week band hai use count kare yaa nahi.. hame kuchh week pahle ki jodi niche lani hai to bichh me aane wala band week gine yaa nahi...

TO MAI BATA DU KI KOI BHI WEEK HO BAND YAA CHALU SABHI GINANE HOTE HAI.. BHALE HI 1 WEEK BAND HO 2 WEEK BAND HO.. YAA 3 MAHINE (aapko pata hoga kab band huaa tha.)...

Par jaruri nahi hai ki satte wale wo week count hi kare..
kyonki aisa pahle bhi huaa hai.. ki un logo ne band week count naa kiya ho...

For example..
12.10.2009 wale week me aise hi... 11 dino ke liye band tha.. tab.. un logo ne band week count nahi kiya tha...
