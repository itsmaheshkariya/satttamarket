KALYAN - MAIN KE LIYE....
Kalyan aur main me kisi bhi ek SCHEME bracket ke aaspass brackets apni position tay karte hai....
Isme hota ye hai.. ki agar koi SCHEME bracket same day par repeat hota hai... to pahli baar me jo hota hai wohi same dusri aur teesri baar bhi us bracket ke aaspass hota hai..

EXAMPLE:

KALYAN

83 63 66 84 31 81
63 00 31 04 70 88
53 21 66 78 66 56
21 18 50 20 23 67

Yaha par 66 base jodi hai. ya scheme jodi hai

yaha ye huaa hai ki-
Pahli baar jab 66 Wednesday ko aayi tab wednesday ke ek din pahle Tuesday ki jodi 63 agele week monday ko aayi... 63

Dusri baar jab 66 wednesday ko aayi tab wednesday ke ek din pahle Tuesday ki jodi 21 gale week monday ko aayi 21
