SARDAAR KE FIGURES NEVER FAIL


Created: 4.11 02:28 by
Views: 66915

Kisi bhi din kalyan aur Main me Achuk ank nikalte hai. Ise sardar ke Figures kahate hai.
Is trick me card me se ank nikalate hai jo achuk pass hote hai....

Isme ye hota hai ki KALYAN ke card ko jodte hai to MAIN ke liye ank nikalate hai, aur MAIN ke card ko jodte hai to agle din Kalyan ke liye ank nikalate hai.....

ye trick is prakar hai

Example:

MAIN KE ANK:
Manlo Kalyan me jodi aayi 37

238 37 179

to isme sabse pahle open ka card jodenge

2+3+8 = 13
aur isi tarh close ke card ko jodenge
1+7+9 = 17
ab dono total ko jodenge
13+17 = 30
to yaha par 2 FIGURE banate hai 3 aur 0 aur inka total bhi jo ki fir se 3 (3+0 =3)hi ho raha hai....
to main ke liye final ank hai...

3 aur 0 <= SARDAR KE FIGURES
