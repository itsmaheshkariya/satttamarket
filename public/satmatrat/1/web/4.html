WEEKLY CALCULATION - FOR ALL MARKETS


Created: 2.11 12:42 by Admin Sir
Views: 135915

Sabhi games me har week koi na koi calculation chalata hai.
ye calculation kuch aasan jod(addition), ghatav(subtraction) aise ganit ke sath hota hai.

ex.
28/05/2007 we week me ye jodiya bani kalyan me

78
15
62
89
31
87

ye week is tarah se aaya

sabse pahle din jodi aayi hai... 78
78 me jodi ko agar joda jaye to 7+8 =15 to is total ka pahle ank hai 1 jo ki agle din open me aaya isi tarah se agle din ki jodi 62 ko total karne par 6+2 =8 agle din open me aaya...


isi tarah close me close ke ank 8 me agar alag se 7 joda jaye to total hota hai 7+8 = 15 isme aakhri ka ank hai 5 jo ki agle din close me aaya isi tarah se agle din ki jodi 62 ke close ke ank 2 me fir se 7 joda jaye to total hota hai 2+7 = 9 jo ki agle din close me aaya...


(=====) 78 (====)
(7+8=15) 15 (8+7 =15)
(1+5 = 6) 62 (5+7 =12)
(6+2 = 8) 89 (2+7 = 9)
